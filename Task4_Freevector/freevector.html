what is an anaesthetist?

an anesthatist is a specialist doctor,like the surgeon who
has completed a meidcal degree at university
for between (5-7 years) and then done a hospital training
at average (8-9) years specifically in anaesthesia,
intensive care emergency,pain and and peri-operative medicine.
they are highly trained to look after you during surgery.
Your anasthetist will watch your condition carefully until
you have safely awaken in the recovery room.
